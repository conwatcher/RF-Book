# Red Foundations

<p class="micro">Science Fiction - Series Book 1</p>

<div class="grid">
  <div class="card" style="grid-column: span 7;">
    <h2>Premise</h2>
    <p>
      In a future where an anomalous impact initiates rapid terraforming on Mars, humanity seizes a once-impossible chance:
      become a multi-planetary species. The first decade of colonization tests every assumption about survival, governance,
      and what "independence" really means.
    </p>
    <p class="micro">
      Central tension: a struggle for self-governance - both from Earth and from the very systems designed to ensure survival.
    </p>

    <div class="hr"></div>

    <h2>Core Themes</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Independence vs. dependency</li>
      <li>The nature of control</li>
      <li>Building a new society from scratch</li>
      <li>Human resilience and adaptation</li>
    </ul>
