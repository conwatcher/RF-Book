      <h1>About P.J. Cereste</h1>
      <p>P.J. Cereste writes hard science fiction about the moments when civilization has to decide what it actually believes — about governance, about freedom, about what human beings owe each other when the old rules no longer apply.</p>
      <p><em>Red Foundations</em> is the first book in a planned series set during the first decade of Mars colonization. It is the story of the engineers who built the colony, the administrators caught between two worlds, the scientists sitting on discoveries that could change everything, and the quiet, accumulating pressure of a population that has stopped thinking of itself as a branch office of Earth.</p>
      <p>The series is built on rigorous science, grounded political realism, and the conviction that the most interesting stories happen not in the moments of action, but in the decisions that make action inevitable.</p>
      <p class="pen-name-note">P.J. Cereste is a pen name used intentionally. The work speaks for itself.</p>
